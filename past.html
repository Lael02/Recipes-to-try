Pastelitos de guayaba (Cuban guava pastries)

12 pastelitos | Time: ∼ 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 170g guava paste
  • 2 puff pastry, store bought
  • 1 egg
  • 50g cream cheese (optional)
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 190 degrees celcius.
  2. Let puff pastry out of the frigde and let it reach room temperature.
  3. Slice the guava paste into 1/4”-thick slices.
  4. Beat the egg in a small bowl and set aside.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the thawed puff pastry to about 1/8” thick.
  6. Cut the puff pastry into equal squares and repeat the process with the second pastry sheet.
  8. Place quares into a baking sheet and put the guava slices and cream cheese(optional)in the middle and fold.
  9. Repeat the process.
  10. Brush the egg wash on top of the pastries and bake for 15 minutes until puffed up and golden.
  11. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of the pastries and enjoy.
