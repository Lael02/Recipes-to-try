Bolo de cenoura (Brazilian carrot cate)

1 medium cake | Time: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g carrots, sliced
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 150ml oil
  • 200g flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Cacao, to your liking

Instructions

  1. Put the oil, eggs, sugar, and carrots ito a blender and blend the mixture till smooth (approx. 3 min).
  2. Preheat oven (180 degrees).
  3. Place mixture into a bowl and add flour. Mix gently.
  4. Add baking powder and mix it.
  5. Grease the cake mold and place the cake mixture.
  6. Place the cake in the oven at 175 degrees (celcius) for 45 minutes.
  7. Brigadeiro: Place the condensed milk into a pan with the butter and cacao. cook it in medium heat until it reaches a thick consistency.
  8. Allow the brigadeiro to cool and place it after removing the cake and allowing it to cool.
  9. Enjoy.
