Mufete

2 servings | Time: 45+ minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g cooked butter beans
  • 100ml tomato pulp
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 vine tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 medium fish
  • 250g sweet potato, cooked
  • 250g cassava, cooked
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the beans. In a pan, saut√© a chopped onion and garlic in 3 tablespoons of olive oil.
  2. Add the cooked beans, bay leaf and tomato pulp. Season with salt and pepper and cook over low heat for 15 minutes.
  3. In a salad bowl, add the chopped pepper, tomato and cucumber. Drizzle with lemon juice, three tablespoons of olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Mix and set aside.
  4. Heat a grill or some coals and cook the fish seasoned with salt.
  5. Drizzle the fish with the remaining olive oil and sprinkle with parsley.
  6. Serve with beans, salad, sweet potatoes, cassava and banana bread.
