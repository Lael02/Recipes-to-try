Bibimbap

1 Serving | Time: ∼ 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 100g of Cooked Korean short-grain/jasmine/sushi rice
  • 50g of thinly sliced beef
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber/zucchini, julienned
  • 25g of spinach
  • 25g of bean sprouts
  • 37g of mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • spring onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the thinly sliced ribeye to the marinade and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, preferably overnight, in the refrigerator.
  3. Sear the marinated ribeye in a pan until cooked through and slightly browned, then set aside.
  4. In the same pan, sauté the carrot, zucchini, and mushroom slices separately with a bit of sesame oil until tender, then set aside.
  5. Blanch the spinach in boiling water, drain, and season with a little sesame oil and salt.
  6. Blanch the bean sprouts briefly and season with salt.
  7. Put the rice in a bowl and arrage the vegetables on top of the rice.
  8. Fry the egg and place it on the bowl.
  9. Mix all sauce ingredients well and add some ketchup if desired to reduce spiciness.
  10. Sprinkle gochujang, sesame seeds, and spring onions over the Bibimbap and serve immediately.
  11. Dont forget: Everyone can customize their Bibimbap as desired. Enjoy!
